Want the best? Got to wait!

In: Uncategorized

5 Aug 2009

Well – I thought it hurt yesterday – today was a whole different ballpark. I had been planning on walking to the hospital (roughly 3 km away) but after I woke up – all those thoughts went out the door.

The staff and doctor were nice, but there was some glum news – the surgery will be in six months. I could go to another doctor and get it done much sooner – but if you want the best, you gotta wait. The one upside is that I will just have one surgery now.

The other silver lining is this gives me lots of time to be at peak physical condition. The stronger I am, the easier post-op will be.

Well – we kinda know

In: The Start

30 Jul 2009

Got back to Toronto last night – and immediately went to the doctor first thing in the morning.

After feeling up the knee, he referred me to a sports clinic. Personally called to book me an appointment the same day.

I ended up going to Athletic Edge Sports Medicine at 121 King Street West. The staff was very helpful, and the doctor came across as both knowledgeable and helpful. He played around with my knee, compared how ‘loose’ my right (injured) was to my left, and so forth. We also talked about what potentially happened and what the potential solutions would be. One thing he did mention is that even in the last 9 years knee surgery has come a long way. I knew this already being a basketball nut.

His basic understanding is that I likely did tear my ACL (due to my previous history), and possibly re-injured the same meniscus (since it was never surgically repaired). The MRI request has been sent over to the hospital, and we can figure out what to do once the results are back.

I can now walk around pretty much fine, but the knee lets me know pretty quickly that it doesn’t like me much.

Oh shit – Injured Again

In: The Start

9 Jan 2009

Well that sucked.

Seems like I’ve hurt my knee badly again. Was playing soccer when I shot at goal and heard a nasty pop. Mind you – this was very casual soccer. Teams of 6, co-ed, no slide tackling, and across the field. It was snowing, but I had been warming up.
